Spicy Fusion Hotpot Sauce

A bold and energetic sauce with hints of sweetness, perfect for those who enjoy vibrant dance music and electronic beats.

Flavor Profile

  • Top Genre: Electronic
  • Energy: High
  • Mood: Upbeat

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp Chili oil
  • 1 tbsp Sesame oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorns
  • 1 stalk Green onion, chopped

Instructions

  1. Mix soy sauce, chili oil, and sesame oil in a small bowl.
  2. Add minced garlic and honey, stir until combined.
  3. Crush Sichuan peppercorns and add to the mixture.
  4. Sprinkle chopped green onions as garnish.
  5. Let the flavors meld for 5 minutes before using.
